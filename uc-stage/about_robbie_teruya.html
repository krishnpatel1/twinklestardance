About Robbie

Prior to joining Tiffany's Dance Academy in 2003, Robbie had spent her professional career working in fundraising and volunteer administration for such organizations as Stage One, The LA Regional Foodbank, Harvey Mudd College and the American Heart Association. She has worked alongside Paul and Tiffany for more than 10 years, participating in the earliest evolutions of CostumeManager.com and helping TDA grow. Today she manages the 300+ student Performing Company and Human Resources, Recital and Nutcracker production and staff management.
