About Christine

Christine Dion brings with her more than 30 years in the beauty business. She has worked as a make-up artist internationally for fashion magazines, commercials, television, fashion shows & dance theater. She became popular in the Dance industry from her monthly beauty and advice columns in Dance Spirit and Dance Teacher Magazine. Today Christine writes for several industry magazines, is a published author "High Performance Beauty" (Princeton Press) and speaks across the country at conferences, conventions, dance and theatrical studios.
