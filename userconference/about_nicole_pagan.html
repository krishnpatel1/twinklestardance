About Nicole

Nicole began her dance training at Darlene’s Dance ‘N Such and continued her education at Humboldt State University. After graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Dance Studies, she traveled to New York where she won a scholarship to the Alvin Ailey Summer Intensive Program. While in New York, Nicole worked on several dance projects as well as shooting a pilot episode for the reality hip-hop TV show “In ‘da Club.” Upon returning to California, Nicole danced with Ross Dance Company, Soul Conspiracy, and Chapkis Dance.
