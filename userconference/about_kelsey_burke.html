About Kelsey

Kelsey is an experienced teacher/choreographer/dancer, has trained extensively in ballet, jazz, tap and modern dance, and has been working with TDA for two years teaching and choreographing for all levels and ages including Twinkle Star Dance. Kelsey received her BFA in Dance from the University of Arizona, was the Assistant Director of a local dance studio during her time in Tucson, and later went on to perform with Ballet Austin's professional company in Austin, TX.
